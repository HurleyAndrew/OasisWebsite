The Problem
Stress in the modern world is a constant.
Constant stress takes a toll on the body, brain, mind, and soul. It can cause a variety of symptoms that affect your overall physical and mental health and well-being.
Statistics
75%
of adults experienced moderate to high stress levels in the past month.
80%
of people are stressed or feel stressed in their work environment.
Our Mission
We are creating a stress reduction space at Imagine RIT.
Our goal is to create an exhibit showcasing how meditation, like breathing exercises, aids in reducing stress, and offer Imagine RIT visitors a chance to sit down and relax from the hectic day. Our exhibit will be employing the 4-7-8 breathing technique, where you inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7 seconds, and exhale for 8 seconds.
The Experience
Personalized and peaceful.
After taking a relaxation quiz, we will be using pulse sensors and respiration belts to track visitors biometrics while they follow the meditaiton. During the breathing exercise a visual will build based around the users quiz answers and health data. Afterwards they have the opportunity to relive the experience through a web application version of the meditation.
