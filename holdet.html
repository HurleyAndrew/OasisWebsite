The Problem
Stress in the modern world is a constant.
Constant stress takes a toll on the body, brain, mind, and soul. It can cause a variety of physical symptoms that affect your overall health and well-being.

Mission
Reducing Stress
We will be employing the 4-7-8 breathing technique in our exhibit experience. This is a breathing exercise where you inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7 seconds and exhale for 8 seconds.
Meditation
4-7-8 Breathing Technique
We are providing a space for Imagine RIT visitors to take a moment out of the busy to sit and breathe. Using visitor vitals and few answers to a quiz we will produce a personalized experience and visualization.
Solution
Visualization Experience
We are creating a visualization showcasing the positive effects that breathing exercises have on the body to aid in reducing stress.

Floor Plan
Visitors can enter the room to either side, viewing information about relaxation, stress, etc., and interacting with the projection. At the stations they can participate in the guided meditation breathing exercise.
Isometric View
A more detialed 3D rendering of the room. From here you can see the room layout in greater detail including the meditation stations and projection system.
Kinect Shilouette Installation
As visitors walk into and by the exhibit they can see themselves visualized on screen like a mirror.
